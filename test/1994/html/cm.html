Cellar Management


Cellar management can be performed either with or without the Visual Cellar capability. Use of the Visual Cellar involves several steps:
  • Specify the storage facilities within your cellar (click here for more details)
  • Add those wines in your cellar not contained in the wine database
  • Indicate which wines are stored in each bin(s) within the Visual Cellar
  • Input any personal information (cost paid, supplier, date purchased)

You can also maintain your cellar without the need to specify the physical storage and indicate which wines are in which bin(s). This approach involves less "up front" effort. On the other hand, you will not be able to visualize your cellar on the computer screen or get visual search results.

You can indicate which wines are in which bins within the Visual Cellar by "drag and drop" from the wine database or by manually inputting the information.

If you don't maintain a Visual Cellar, you can still enter all the cellar information shown. In addition to the price paid, current value, supplier, date purchased and cellar location, two fields are provided for storing other information (such as insurance codes or the owner of the bottle, if you store wines for others).
