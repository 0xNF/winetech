The Main Form


This is the entire Main Form used to: 1) find wines of interest, 2) display virtually all of the information on file about a particular wine (one wine at a time), and 3) perform most cellar management tasks.

The Main Form is divided into color keyed areas, each of which focuses on one of these tasks. The Green area is used to find wines. The Blue area displays information about a wine, once it is found. There are two Purple areas which are used for cellar management. One is shown here. The other replaces the Blue area when the button labeled "Show Bottle Fields" is depressed. This second area is shown in the discussion of Cellar Management.

Should you click on many parts of the image shown below you will be taken to a discussion of that particular capability.
