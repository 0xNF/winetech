Finding Multiple Vintages
You can easily retrieve wines for multiple vintage years or a range of years in one operation using the Find Form. If you type a series of vintage years - as shown to the left - the software will find wines from any of the years you type.




You can also type a range of years such as:

  •         -1990 (All vintages up to and including 1990)
  • 1990 -        (All vintages from 1990 and beyond)
  • 1980-1989 (All vintages from 1980 through 1989)
