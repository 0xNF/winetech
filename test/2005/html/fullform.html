This is the entire Wine Form used to: 1) find wines of interest, 2) display virtually all of the information on file about a particular wine (one wine at a time), and 3) perform most cellar management tasks.

The Wine Form is divided into color keyed areas, each of which focuses on one of these tasks. The Green area is used to find wines. The Blue area displays information about a wine, once it is found. There are two Dark Red areas which are used for cellar management. One is shown here. The other replaces the Blue area when the button labeled "Show Bottle Fields" is depressed. This second area is shown in the discussion of Cellar Management. You can click on that button and some of the others to see where they would take you.
